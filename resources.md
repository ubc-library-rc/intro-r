### Tips

**On the editor**

`CTRL`+`enter` runs the command in the current line (`cmd`+`enter` on MacOS)

`CTRL`+`w` closes current tab (`cmd`+`w` on MacOS)

After typing the first few lines of a command, `tab` auto-completes the command
